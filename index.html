Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pet hair remover

How does the reusable pet hair remover work?

Our pet hair remover uses micro-bristle technology to attract and collect pet hair from various surfaces. The roller collects hair in an internal chamber that can be easily emptied when full. It works through electrostatic attraction and doesn't require any sticky sheets or refills.

How do I clean the roller?

Simply press the release button to open the chamber, remove the collected hair, and close it again. For a deeper clean, you can rinse the roller with mild soap and water, but make sure it's completely dry before using it again.

Will it work on all types of pet hair?

Yes! Our roller is designed to work effectively on short, long, and even stubborn pet hair from dogs, cats, and other furry pets. It's especially effective on furniture, clothing, and car interiors.

How long does the roller last?

With proper care, your FurGone roller should last for years. There are no parts that wear out or need replacement under normal use, making it a sustainable and economical choice for pet owners.
